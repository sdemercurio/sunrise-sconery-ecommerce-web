How To Order

dozen or half dozen?

Select whether you want a dozen or half-dozen.

choose your scones

Our scones are baked fresh daily and can be delivered straight to your doorstep. Have an allergy or food intolerance? No problem! We offer gluten-free and dairy-free. Stay tuned for sugar-free!

schedule your delivery

We offer same day delivery but encourage orders a day in advance. For orders larger than 2 dozen, we ask you place these orders at least 3 days in advance to allow us time to ensure proper inventory.

get happy!

We have many happy customers and we want to make you happy too.

To Place Your Order
